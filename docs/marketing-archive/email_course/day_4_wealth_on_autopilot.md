**The secret used by the wealthiest families in the world is called Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA).**

Instead of trying to buy a massive amount of gold all at once, you buy a small, consistent amount every single month, regardless of where the price is.

- When prices are lower, your monthly budget buys **more** physical metal.
- When prices are higher, your existing stack increases in value.

Over time, this completely irons out market volatility, lowers your average cost per ounce, and builds an incredibly formidable physical treasure chest — without you ever having to stress about charts.
