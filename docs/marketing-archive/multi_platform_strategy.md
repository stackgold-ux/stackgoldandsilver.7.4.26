## 7. Operational Standards & Multi-Channel Brand Cohesion
Regardless of which platform a customer enters through, our operational commitment remains unchanged:
1.  **Spot Price Integrity:** All bullion offerings are pinned dynamically to real-time mint-sourced spot prices with a flat, transparent 15% markup.
2.  **Anonymous & Secure Fulfillment:** All physical metals are packed in double-boxed, discreet, un-marked, and fully insured shipping boxes directly managed by the CEO.
3.  **Cross-Platform Database Sync:** Utilizing our Headless Wix API layer and synchronized Turso SQLite pipeline to ensure orders and subscription statuses are updated in real-time across all client touchpoints.
