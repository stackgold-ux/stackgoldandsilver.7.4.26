## 2. Platform Positioning & Best Practices

To maximize organic reach, each of our target platforms must be utilized according to its native audience psychology and technical algorithms.

### 🥈 Facebook: The Trust & Family Anchor
*   **Target Audience:** Parents, heads of households, grandparents, and community-minded planners (Ages 30–65+).
*   **Content Tone:** Sincere, protective, long-term legacy planning, and security-oriented.
*   **Best Practices:** 
    *   Post longer, text-rich captions that encourage comments and community stories.
    *   Share horizontal and square videos that focus on family security and asset protection.
    *   Actively reply to every comment with trust-affirming, empathetic copy.

### 📸 Instagram: Visual Desirability & Lifestyle
*   **Target Audience:** Modern wealth-builders, young professionals, and style-conscious stackers (Ages 22–45).
*   **Content Tone:** Clean, high-fidelity, aspirational, and aesthetic.
*   **Best Practices:**
    *   Deploy high-quality Reels showing the actual shine of physical metals, custom laser-engraving closeups, and premium Stack Swag streetwear.
    *   Use Instagram Stories daily with "Link Stickers" leading directly to the checkouts.
    *   Utilize "Polls" and "Q&A" stickers (e.g., "Silver or Gold?") to train the algorithm for high engagement.

### 🧵 Threads: Intellectual Stacking & Sound Money Debates
*   **Target Audience:** Tech-savvy readers, financial contrarians, and text-first thinkers (Ages 18–40).
*   **Content Tone:** Conversational, intellectually provocative, analytical, and fast-paced.
*   **Best Practices:**
    *   Write punchy, text-only "threads" breaking down complex economic traps (like inflation or fractional reserve banking) in simple terms.
    *   Engage directly in the comments of larger personal finance accounts with respectful, value-first counterarguments.
    *   Include a single, clean link to the blog or subscription at the end of a high-value thread.

### 🛡️ Truth Social: Sovereign Security & Financial Liberty
*   **Target Audience:** Patriotic savers, anti-inflation advocates, and liberty-minded household heads (Ages 25–65+).
*   **Content Tone:** Strong-willed, sovereign, protective of personal liberty, and critical of central banks.
*   **Best Practices:**
    *   Focus heavily on the "Wealth Shield" concept—protecting family assets from printing presses and government overreach.
    *   Use bold, direct-response copy that highlights self-custody and hard money.
    *   Engage with hashtags like `#SoundMoney`, `#GoldStandard`, `#InflationShield`, and `#BuyPhysical`.

### 🎵 TikTok: High-Energy Edutainment & Viral Hooks
*   **Target Audience:** Gen Z and Millennial builders, first-time stackers, and visual learners (Ages 18–35).
*   **Content Tone:** High energy, educational, fast-cut, humorous, and highly authentic.
*   **Best Practices:**
    *   Use the "3-Second Rule": Start with an explosive physical visual (e.g., dropping a heavy 10oz bar on a desk) or a highly provocative verbal hook.
    *   Focus on quick, narrative-driven history lessons (e.g., "The Nazi Gold Train" or "How to trade the Gold-Silver ratio").
    *   Utilize trending audio background tracks at low volume to ride the algorithm's waves.

### 👻 Snapchat: Behind-the-Scenes & High-Urgency Drops
*   **Target Audience:** Younger, active, mobile-first audiences (Ages 15–28).
*   **Content Tone:** Raw, unfiltered, immediate, and spontaneous.
*   **Best Practices:**
    *   Publish raw, vertical phone videos of orders being packed inside the warehouse (packing "secret unboxing surprises" like micro-grains or apparel stickers).
    *   Run high-urgency 24-hour flash promos (e.g., "Use code STACK25 on our bio link for first-month subscription perks").
    *   Highlight the immediate, satisfying "clink" of coins and bars in casual, everyday settings.
