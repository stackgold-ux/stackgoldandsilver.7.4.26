## Part 5: "Road to 99" Official Rules & Legal Transparency
*This text ensures absolute compliance, legal transparency, and builds incredible brand credibility. To be published on our dedicated landing page `/rules` and linked in the checkout footer.*

### 1. Eligibility
The "Road to 99" Campaign & Grand Giveaway Challenge is open to legal residents of the United States who are eighteen (18) years of age or older at the time of entry. Void where prohibited by law. Employees, officers, and directors of Stack Your Gold™ and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate.

### 2. Campaign Period
*   **The "Every 9th Customer Surprise Stack" Promotion:** Begins immediately upon launch and runs continuously until the 99th active, verified Stack Squad™ subscriber is enrolled.
*   **The 9/9/26 Grand Giveaway Challenge:** Begins immediately and concludes at 11:59 PM EST on September 1, 2026. The random drawing will take place on September 9, 2026 (9/9/26).

### 3. How to Enter
*   **Automatic Entry via Purchase:** Participants receive one (1) automatic entry into the Grand Giveaway for every one dollar ($1.00 USD) spent on physical merchandise or active subscription payments on StackYourGold.com during the eligibility window.
*   **Alternative Method of Entry (AMOE - No Purchase Necessary):** To enter the Grand Giveaway without making a purchase, send a hand-printed, self-addressed stamped postcard containing your full name, email address, physical shipping address, phone number, and a 100-word handwritten statement on "Why physical sound money matters for my family's future" to: *Stack Your Gold, Attn: Road to 99 Giveaway, 100 Sovereign Way, Suite 300, Miami, FL 33101*. Limit of one (1) mail-in entry per person, which shall be counted as equivalent to one hundred (100) automatic purchase entries. All mail-in entries must be postmarked by August 25, 2026, and received by September 1, 2026.

### 4. The "Surprise Stack" Selection Process
Every new, active subscriber sequence is logged in our Turso SQL transaction database in real-time. Whenever the subscriber counter hits a multiple of nine (e.g., #9, #18, #27, #36, #45, #54, #63, #72, #81, #90, and #99), a special digital badge is triggered. The fulfillment team, directly overseen by our CEO, will manually place a customized physical "Surprise Stack" item inside that shipment before it is double-boxed, insured, and dispatched.

### 5. Viral Unboxing Multipliers
Participants who have completed a purchase or have registered an entry can multiply their active entries by sharing a raw, authentic unboxing video of their Stack Your Gold™ delivery:
*   **Double (2X) Entries:** Share the video on one (1) social media platform, tag `@StackYourGold`, and use `#StackYourGold`.
*   **Triple (3X) Entries:** Share the video on three (3) or more social media platforms (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, and X), tag `@StackYourGold` on all, and use `#StackYourGold`.
*   *Validation:* To claim the multiplier, participants must email a direct link or screenshot of their social posts to `verification@stackyourgold.com` with their original order number. Multipliers will be applied to the entry database within forty-eight (48) hours of verification.

### 6. Drawing & Winner Notification
The Grand Giveaway drawing will be conducted on **September 9, 2026**. One (1) Grand Prize winner will be selected via a randomized digital drawing from all verified entries. The winner will be notified via email and phone within twenty-four (24) hours of the drawing. If a winner cannot be contacted or fails to claim the prize within seven (7) business days, an alternate winner will be randomly drawn.

### 7. Prize Delivery & Logistics
All prizes—including Surprise Stacks and Grand Giveaway items—consist of real, physical physical gold, silver, or brand merchandise. Prizes are fully insured and shipped discreetly to the winner's verified physical address. Outlying shipping, logistics, and insurance fees are covered 100% by Stack Your Gold™.
